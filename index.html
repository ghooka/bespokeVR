Ace your video interviews & meetings

These days everyone has to do video meetings and interviews. Increasingly our interviewer is an AI-enabled machine. We feel anxious and awkward. Nobody tells us what works well to get the ‘Yes’. The MvM app will give you expert training and rehearsal so you ace your interviews and get the Yes!

About us

Who we are

02

Our Mission

Our mission is to level the playing field in favour of people in a world that is increasingly shaped in favour of AI-enabled machines.

03

What we do

Large organisations, in both the private and public sectors, are rapidly implementing artificial intelligence enabled, machine interviews of candidates and of applicants for services like bank loans.

The MvM App

The MvM App rehearses and prepares you for your critical interviews and meetings to help you get the Yes! whether you are interviewed by a machine or a human.

Recommended use

Use the MvM App every day for at least 20 minutes a day for a minimum of 5 days before an important business meeting or interview.

Technical Requirements

The current version of the App is designed to operate on a smartphone operating iOS 13.6.0 or later version of iOS.
